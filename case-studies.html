The Challenge

Apex Manufacturing was experiencing an average of 6+ hours of unplanned downtime per month, costing the company an estimated $45,000 annually in lost productivity. Their 20-year-old server infrastructure was failing regularly, and their single part-time IT consultant was overwhelmed. The manufacturing floor was increasingly connected to their network via IoT devices, creating significant security vulnerabilities that nobody was managing.

Our Solution

Keystone Technologies conducted a comprehensive infrastructure audit and developed a phased transformation plan that minimized disruption to manufacturing operations.

  • Replaced aging server infrastructure with Azure-backed hybrid cloud
  • Deployed next-generation firewall with IoT network segmentation
  • Implemented 24/7 monitoring via ConnectWise RMM platform
  • Enrolled all endpoints in SentinelOne EDR protection
  • Migrated 85 users to Microsoft 365 with Teams collaboration
  • Established automated backup with 4-hour RTO guarantee

The Results

Within 90 days of full deployment, unplanned downtime dropped to near zero. The predictable flat-rate managed service replaced unpredictable repair bills, reducing total IT spend by 40%. The manufacturing team now has confidence that their technology will perform reliably every shift.

"The transformation has been incredible. We haven't had a significant IT outage in over two years. Our team focuses on manufacturing, not fighting computers. Keystone has been one of the best investments we've ever made."

Our Solution

  • Emergency security assessment and immediate MFA deployment
  • Advanced email security with AI-powered phishing detection
  • SentinelOne EDR deployed on all endpoints
  • Security awareness training with simulated phishing campaigns
  • 24/7 MDR service for continuous threat monitoring
  • Wire transfer approval process redesign

The Results

Zero security incidents since implementation. Simulated phishing click rate dropped from 34% to 2% after training. The comprehensive security stack has blocked numerous attempted intrusions. Estimated $850,000 in potential fraud losses avoided.

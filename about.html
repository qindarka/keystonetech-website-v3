Our Story

Built on Trust. Driven by Excellence.

Since 2009, Keystone Technologies has been the IT backbone of Ontario's most successful businesses. Here's how we got here.

Our History

15 Years of Excellence in IT

Keystone Technologies was founded in 2009 with a simple but powerful belief: Ontario's small and medium businesses deserve the same quality of IT support as enterprise corporations — without the enterprise price tag.

Our founder, John W., had spent years watching businesses suffer from reactive, break-fix IT that was costing them productivity and growth. He built Keystone around a proactive model — where technology problems are prevented, not just fixed.

Today, we serve 200+ businesses across Ontario, from London to Toronto to Kitchener-Waterloo. We've grown from a 3-person team to a 25-strong organization of certified engineers, security experts, and cloud specialists. But our commitment to personal service has never changed.

Cybersecurity

Multi-Layered Security That Actually Works

Traditional antivirus is dead. Modern cyber threats require behavioral detection, AI-powered analysis, and human expertise working together.

Our cybersecurity stack combines best-in-class tools with expert management, giving you enterprise-grade protection without needing an in-house SOC team.

Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)

+

EDR goes far beyond antivirus. It monitors endpoint behavior in real-time, detecting suspicious activity that traditional tools miss. Using AI and machine learning, we identify and neutralize threats before they cause damage.

Managed Detection & Response (MDR)

+

MDR adds 24/7 human expert monitoring on top of EDR. Our security analysts investigate alerts, hunt for threats, and respond to incidents — all without you needing an in-house security team.

Security Awareness Training

+

95% of breaches involve human error. Our training programs use simulated phishing attacks and engaging modules to turn your employees from your biggest vulnerability into your strongest defense.

Vulnerability Assessments

+

We regularly scan your entire environment for vulnerabilities — open ports, unpatched software, misconfigured systems. Findings are prioritized by risk and remediation is handled by our team.

Incident Response Planning

+

When (not if) a security incident occurs, having a plan makes all the difference. We develop, document, and regularly test your incident response plan so your team knows exactly what to do.

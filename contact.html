Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

How long does a free IT assessment take?

+

Our free IT assessment typically takes 30–60 minutes. We'll do a brief discovery call to understand your current setup and challenges, then review your key systems and provide a written report of our findings and recommendations within 2 business days.

Do you have a minimum contract term?

+

Our standard managed services agreements are 12 months, which allows us to plan and deliver consistent service. However, we also offer month-to-month options. We'll recommend the right fit based on your needs during the discovery call.

How quickly can you respond to IT emergencies?

+

For managed services clients, our average response time for critical issues is under 15 minutes, 24/7. Emergency on-site dispatch is typically within 2 hours in the London area. For new clients with a business emergency, call our emergency line at (519) 555-9999.

What industries do you specialize in?

+

We serve businesses across all industries in Ontario, with particular expertise in healthcare, legal, financial services, manufacturing, professional services, and retail. Our compliance knowledge covers PIPEDA, healthcare privacy, and financial regulations.

Can you work with our existing systems?

+

Yes, absolutely. We start by understanding and optimizing what you have. If replacements are needed, we provide honest recommendations without upselling. We work with all major vendors and technologies, from Microsoft to Cisco to custom line-of-business applications.

How is pricing structured?

+

Our managed services are priced as a flat monthly fee per user or device, giving you complete cost predictability. No surprise bills for routine support. Project work (migrations, deployments) is quoted separately. We'll provide a detailed proposal after the free assessment.

Do you serve businesses outside London?

+

Yes! We serve businesses across Ontario, including Kitchener-Waterloo, Windsor, Sarnia, Stratford, and Toronto. Remote support is available province-wide. On-site support is primarily focused in southwestern Ontario.

Not Ready for a Full Assessment?

That's fine. Give us a call and let's have a casual conversation about your IT. No forms, no commitment.
